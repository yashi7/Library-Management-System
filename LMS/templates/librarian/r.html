RULES AND REGULATION

BORROWING RULES

  • Each member can borrow a maximum of 5 books at a time.
  • Books must be returned within 2 weeks from the date of borrowing.
  • Renewals are allowed for a maximum of 2 times per book.
  • Reference books cannot be borrowed and must be used within the library premises.
  • Fines will be imposed for late returns.

CODE OF CONDUCT

  • All visitors are required to maintain silence within the library premises.
  • Eating or drinking is not allowed in the library.
  • Mobile phones must be on silent mode and calls should be taken outside the library.
  • Respect the library materials and infrastructure.
  • Computer usage is limited to educational purposes only.

MEMBERSHIP RULES

  • Library membership is open to students, faculty, and staff of the institution.
  • A valid ID card is required for membership registration.
  • Membership renewal must be done annually.
  • Lost or damaged books must be compensated according to the library's policy.

INTERNET USAGE

  • Internet usage is provided for research and educational purposes only. Inappropriate use of the internet is strictly prohibited.
  • Downloading, accessing inappropriate content, or engaging in any illegal activities are strictly forbidden.
