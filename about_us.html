Christ University Library

About Us

CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Pune Lavasa Campus has two main libraries, one is a central library and the other is a management library

Management Library: The management library is located on the second floor of the Management Block. The library is spread across 4992 square feet and has a comprehensive collection of books and journals related to management. The management library has a good collection of textbooks, reference books, periodicals, journal archives etc. The library also has facilities like internet and Wi-Fi service symposium room for group discussion, locker facility, computer lab, reprography facility, current affairs section etc. The library subscribes to multiple electronic resources from EBSCO, ProQuest, J-Gate etc. for its students and faculty through IP access. The library activities are computerized and the catalogues are available online.

Central Library: The central library is mainly for all the Under Graduate & Post Graduate programmes offered in the University (Commerce, Research and law, social science library). The central library has a varied collection of textbooks, reference materials and periodicals. The library has facilities like internet and Wi-Fi services, locker facility, computer lab, newspaper, current affairs section etc. The library subscribes to electronic resources like EBSCO, ProQuest, Manupatra, SSC Online etc. for its students and faculty through IP access which can be accessed from anywhere in the campus.
